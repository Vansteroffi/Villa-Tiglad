A haven of peace facing the Atlantic

Discover the charm of Brittany in this former lighthouse keeper's cottage, newly renovated and facing west on a quiet dead-end street. Directly opposite the Walled City of Concarneau and the marina.

You will live to the rhythm of the tides, watching the fishing and pleasure boats come and go; the sounds of the waves will lull you into absolute calm, even in peak season. A permanent spectacle, constantly changing with the hours and the light.

The house features two private outdoor spaces with a plunging view of the sea: a flowered garden with sun loungers and a dining table, as well as a terrace with outdoor seating.
