Our Story

A haven of peace
facing the Atlantic

Discover the charm of Brittany in this former lighthouse keeper's cottage, nestled close to the sea, entirely renovated, facing west on a quiet dead-end street.

Opposite the Walled City of Concarneau and the marina, Villa Tiglad offers a permanent spectacle, constantly changing with the hours and the light.

You will live to the rhythm of the tides, watching the fishing and pleasure boats come and go; the sound of the waves will lull you into absolute calm, even in peak season.

To Explore

Brittany at your fingertips

⚓

Walled City of Concarneau

A fortified medieval town surrounded by the sea, accessible on foot via the coastal path in 10 minutes from the villa.

⛵

Glénan Archipelago

Crystal clear waters with Caribbean colors, pristine beaches, and a famous international sailing center.

🎨

Pont-Aven

The painters' town where Gauguin stayed, full of art galleries, watermills, and famous local biscuits.

⛪

Quimper

The capital of Breton Cornwall, featuring a Gothic cathedral, traditional pottery, and a lively old town center.

🌊

Pointe du Raz

The westernmost cape of mainland France, with dramatic cliffs battered by the Atlantic waves.

🏘

Locronan

One of the most beautiful villages in France, remarkably preserved with 15th-century granite houses.
